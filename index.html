    Products

    Check out the products we offer

    Parcel Management

    This module digitizes the parcel business by allowing for efficient communications between the courier, sender, and recipient of the parcel. We send SMS alerts to the sender/receiver at different stages of the transit of the parcel.

    The system generates various reports of the parcel business by displaying revenue per clerk, station, and what time of day the parcels arrive

    We are looking to provide a value addition to the sector by providing ability to purchase insurance cover for the parcel

    Cashless Payments

    This module allows for digital payments of fares by passengers and parcels by senders. The system leverages a digital wallet for collections with the monies aggregated automatically and can be wired to recipient bank/Mpesa at end of business

    Sacco Back Office

    This module allows the back-office team to be able to administer reports of the Sacco operational activity e.g. total passengers ferried, revenue, deductions, expenses incurred etc

    Asset Financing

    For customers that leverage our cashless payments module we can provide asset financing to enable them to grow their business. An appraisal is done upon 3-6 months of activity on the platform.

    Loading Management

    This module digitizes the passenger loading into vehicles and capture of Sacco deductions. We notify the owner of the vehicle of the revenue the vehicle made per trip and the expenses/deductions that were incurred

    We are looking to provide a value addition to the sector by providing ability for passengers to purchase accident insurance cover for their trip

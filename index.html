    Parcel Management



    This module digitizes parcel operations by facilitating seamless communication in the courier space to both sender and recipient, enhancing overall efficiency in the parcel business.

    PSV Management



    The module offers the option for either automated or manual deductions. Upon processing, an SMS containing a delivery note, outlining the revenue and deductions conducted by the Saccos or PSV companies, is sent to the vehicle owner. Also, the module enables the recording of expenses such as fuel and oil, triggering an SMS notification to the vehicle owner.

    Luggage Management



    A luggage management module is a specialized module designed to oversee passenger luggage storage, tracking, and organization.

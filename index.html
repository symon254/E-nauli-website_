    Products

    Check out the products we offer

    Parcel Management



    This module digitizes the parcel business by allowing for efficient communications between the courier, sender, and recipient of the parcel.

    Parcel Reports - The system generates various reports of the parcel business by displaying revenue per clerk, station, and what time of day the parcels arrive

    Loading Reports - The system provides real time delivery notes notification to the owner, passenger manifest, Deduction reports, Vehicle Expense Tracker.

    PSV Management



    This module allows for digital payments of fares by passengers and parcels by senders. The system leverages a digital wallet for collections with the monies aggregated automatically and can be wired to recipient bank/Mpesa at end of business

    Luggage Management



    This module allows for digital payments of fares by passengers and parcels by senders. The system leverages a digital wallet for collections with the monies aggregated automatically and can be wired to recipient bank/Mpesa at end of business
